# Break My House

An interactive 3D insurance-risk education game/simulation. Players trigger a
hailstorm, kitchen fire, or fallen tree; see the coverage and typical claim
cost; then compare the visible outcome with preventative measures enabled.

The goal is to make home-risk education memorable for customers, new insurance
agents, and community outreach — not to simulate real-world physics.

## Prevention model

- **Impact-resistant roofing** reduces hail damage only.
- **Smoke detectors + extinguisher** reduce kitchen-fire damage.
- **Trim overhanging branches** or **reinforced roof framing** reduce
  fallen-tree damage. These two protections do not stack yet.

See **PROJECT.md** for the detailed prevention logic, requirements, progress,
and architecture.
